How it Works

Sell your books in 3 easy steps

01

Contact us with contact form

Provide us with:

  • Overall condition the books are in
  • Amount of books you have
  • Material of book if possible i.e hardcover, leather, cloth
  • Span of the years of the book such as: 1912-1950
  • Authors

02

Wait for us to contact you!

We will contact you back with a possible offer or if we need to take look in person. Additionally we might ask for more information. We will agree on a time. We like to buy from entities or people that were going to donate books to a public library or otherwise discard of their books. We do not buy books at retail price. No matter what you saw the book going for.

03

We will meet you.

After an agreement and time have been set up. We will meet you! We couldn't make it easier for you. We will have a representative of the company meet you at your facility and inspect the books. We then will give you cash on the spot. It's that easy!
